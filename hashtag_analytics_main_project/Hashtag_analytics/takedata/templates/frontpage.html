Twitter hashtag analytics

Social media sites like Twitter and Tumblr are becoming increasingly common, to the point where tracking social media posts has become an important tool for following everything from celebrities to companies and political parties. One of the most important tools for people to track twitter discussions is the hashtag, to the point many groups outside twitter have started adopting them or an equivalent tool.

If every tag is thought of as one discussion, then an interesting topic is seeing what discussions overlap, what topics are talked about together, or naturally lead into one another. This site is a tool for following and studying these overlaps.

Twitter limits the number of tweet you may download by account, to a maximum of 1800 tweets found ever 15 minutes. Searches use up tweets in intervals of 100. So, for instance, even if you only find 179 tweets, you've used up 200 tweets of your fifteen minute bandwidth.
